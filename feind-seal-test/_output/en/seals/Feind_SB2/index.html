Legend and translation

ὁ ἅγιος Δημήτριος. / Κύριε βοήθει Νικηφόρῳ πρoτωπροέδρῳ καὶ δουκὶ Πελοπονίσου καὶ Ἑλάδος τῷ Βοτανειάτι.

St Demetrios. Lord, help Nikephoros Botaneiates protoproedros and doux of Peloponnese and Hellas.

Commentary

The seal’s issuer is Nikephoros III Botaneiates, emperor of the Byzantine Empire from 1078 to 1081, at an earlier stage of his career.
